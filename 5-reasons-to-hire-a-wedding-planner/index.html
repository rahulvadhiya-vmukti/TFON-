5 Reasons to Hire a Wedding Planner

WE'RE HERE FOR YOU

Why You Should Hire a Wedding Planner

Recently engaged and not sure if you need a wedding planner? Theres only so much a Pinterest board can do to help you prepare. So let us help! 

Its important to know what youre stepping into when planning the big day because it could quickly become overwhelming. Between keeping track of contracts to the day-of scheduling, planning a wedding is a lot of responsibilityand hiring a professional will take that stress off your shoulders. 

Because let's be honest, theyre not just a wedding planner, but also a 

1.YOUR DESIGNER

First and foremost, if you just got engaged, you probably have a million ideas for how you want your special day to look. And what better way to bring those visions to life than working with a professional? 


A wedding planner has experience with your local vendors and also knows how to create a cohesive design with whatever you bring to the table. 

Here at Infinity, our coordinators and designers use their creative talent combined with experience from real events to draft up personalized inspiration boards for your event, taking your ideas and turning them into the wedding of your dreams.

2.ONE POINT OF CONTACT

Having one person as the point of contact for every need and inquiry helps the planning process and day-of coordination run smoothly trust us! A wedding planner will communicate with all of your vendors for you, schedule your appointments and tastings, act as the manager on duty for your big day, and more, alleviating any confusion and streamlining every process.

And dont worry, here at Infinity we dont limit the number of times you can call, email, or meet with us, so feel free to become our best friend.

3.ORGANIZER EXTRAORDINAIRE

From sending your save the dates to creating your day-of timeline, a wedding planner will keep everything in line and on time. 

If youre a Type A person and like to keep everything on file and neatly organized, more power to you! But if youre not, or you would simply rather someone else handle all the to-dos, contracts, and paperwork, then a wedding planner is just the person you need. Theyll make sure nothing slips through the cracks.

4.PROBLEM SOLVER

Dont sweat the small stuff; thats the planner's job! A wedding planner will be the one to worry about your non-RSVPers, vendor schedules, any missed communication... you name it. 

Your day should be about celebrating the love between you and your partner with your family and friends, not putting out fires or worrying everything is in place!

5.FINANCIAL ADVISOR

Lastly, and most importantly, a wedding planner will act as your financial advisor. From stretching your budget to be the most cost-effective, to keeping up with all of the bills, they can handle it all. 

With us, you wont have to worry about any hidden costs or get caught with a service thats more or less than what you planned.
