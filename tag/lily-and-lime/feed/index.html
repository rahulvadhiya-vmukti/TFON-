Jessica and Jordan had blue summer wedding ceremony and reception with elegant details at The Bell Tower.

The pair met in 2015 during orientation at their new jobs. Shortly after, they realized they had a bit more in common than just their career path! Jordan proposed to Jessica three years later in Hilton Head under the Harbor Town lighthouse.

They decided to share their vows with friends and family in Nashville. Jessica told us they "loved the traditional meets modern, the location, and the historic church setting" of The Bell Tower.

It was important that the day went smoothly, and with a full event team, Jessica didn't have to worry about a thing! The day started with a first look between Jessica and Jordan, followed by first looks with her dad, brother, and grandparents. All were swooning over Jessica's elegant bridal look in her Essense of Australia gown.

Jessica and Jordan later said their traditional vows, between the tall arched windows of The Bell Tower's main space before guests enjoyed a cocktail hour on the patio, complete with chardonnay and passed hors d'oeuvres.

Once guests found their seats for the reception, plated dinner service by the in-house catering team began. Options included fried chicken with green beans and mashed potatoes or roasted pork loin with Brussel sprouts and garlic parmesan potatoes.

Jessica and Jordan ended their night with a secret last dance to a beloved Elvis Presley song before exiting through a glow stick send-off!
