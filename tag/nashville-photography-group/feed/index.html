Today we're wishing Ashley and Chris a very happy five year anniversary! Their charming fall wedding at The Bridge Building was as sweet as can be, with a candy bar to boot!

The day began with an intimate ceremony on the rooftop followed by dinner and dancing.

Deep eggplant and charcoal added drama while lavender uplighting kept the rooftop feeling light and bright all night long.

Guests received lightweight shawls in case they got chilly and indulged in a perfectly purple candy bar because love is sweet. Scroll on for more details from Ashley and Chris' charming fall wedding.
