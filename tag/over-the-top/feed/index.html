BRING IT TO THE TABLE

OUR FAVORITE PATTERNED LINENS

Linens are an essential part of any event and instantly set the scene from the minute your guests arrive. Your tables are where a lot of the main action goes down, so you'll want to take the opportunity to have a little fun and really make them shine!

There to protect furniture from stains and spills, linens are not only aesthetically pleasing but functional as well. Available in endless colors, patterns, fabrics, and sizes, we love seeing which ones our clients pick and how they contribute to their overall design, no matter the vibe.

While our linen love is always strong, we're currently crushing on stylish patterned prints. We love that they bring even more depth and dimension to the table â€” pun most definitely intended.

Scroll on to get inspired by some of our favorite patterned linens from real weddings.

1. Printed Napkins

Not a fan of throwing linens on every table? Let your furniture speak for itself and add a patterned accent napkin for simple, no-fuss style.

From French country inspired napkins provided by La Tavola Linen to black and white gingham napkins from Liberty Party Rental, the options are endless.

Photos by Justin Wright, Sara Bill, & Details Nashville

2. Reception Tables

No matter the theme you're envisioning for your special day, make a major impact by adding a patterned linen to every table.

We have major heart eyes for these sheer, geometric ivory linens from Over The Top.

Not to mention, these rich black linens with shimmering metallic accents from BBJ Linen adding effortless drama and sophistication to Stephanie and Drew's modern glam wedding reception at The Bridge Building.

Photos by Sara Bill, Jordan and Alaina, Caley Newberry, & Jen and Chris Creed

3. Accent Tables

Love patterned linens but afraid of committing to every single table? No sweat!

Opt for solid linens in the color of your choice and pick complementary patterned linens for your decor tables instead.

We love accenting the dessert table or guest book table for a fun and unexpected pop!

Photos by Details Nashville, SheHeWe & Taylor Lord
