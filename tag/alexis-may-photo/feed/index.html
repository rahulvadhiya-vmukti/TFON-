Gray and Trent celebrated their love with a romantic and retro-inspired ceremony and reception filled with pink and red details at The Bell Tower.

On their big day, Gray was a true vision in her fitted lace gown with a deep V neckline, padded shoulders, and lace sleeves. She wore her hair in an old Hollywood-style blowout with pearl drop earrings and held a unique bouquet of flamingo flower anthuriums. Her bridesmaids looked elegant and edgy in mismatched black gowns with smaller versions of Gray’s bridal bouquet created with anthurium flowers in white, pink, and red. 

Trent wore a dapper dark red jacquard tuxedo jacket while his groomsmen wore matching classic black. They also brought a bit of Western flare with cowboy hats. 

The ceremony was the definition of romantic and retro with touches of modern details. The Bell Tower’s main space was set with mixed seating featuring vintage-style pink velvet sofas in the first few rows and a modern acrylic stand setup serving as the ceremony backdrop. The stands were adorned with bright pink, red, and white blooms that complemented the pink sofas perfectly. 

Gray and Trent's romantic reception was set with red linens, vintage lounge groupings, and string lighting. Their sweetheart table stood out in white with the most beautiful full blooms and pink taper candles, using their ceremony decor as the backdrop. The retro style continued into their cake which was pink and featured Lambeth-style buttercream decorations in red and white. Not a detail was missed throughout their entire day!
