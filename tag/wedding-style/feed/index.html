DON'T SAY "I DO" WITHOUT YOUR BFF

HOW TO MAKE YOUR PET PART OF YOUR BIG DAY

Is there anything better than pets in weddings? We love to see how our real couples celebrate their families in their weddings at The Bridge Building and The Bell Tower, especially when there's a four-legged member who gets to take center stage!

From using your furry friend as a flower girl to incorporating your dog into your decor, there are plenty of fun ways to make pets an essential part of your wedding day.

Scroll on for some heartwarming and Pinterest-worthy inspiration from our real weddings!

1. The Wedding Pawty

Why leave your four-legged friend at home when you can dress them up and have them walk down the aisle? Give your pet a little bowtie, collar corsage, flower crown, or even a little doggie dress or tuxedo. Your guests are guaranteed to have a great time during the ceremony with a pet in attendance, plus it makes for some truly priceless photos. There's no better best man than your adopted best friend!

Photos by Julie Paisley,  Rachel Moore,  Abbey Hunter, Caley Newberry, and Blue Genes Photography.

2. Purrfectly Topped Cakes

Wedding cake toppers or groom's cakes are a perfect way to make a sweet tribute to your pet! Go for a laser-cut silhouette that will become a keepsake, sweet little figurines of your pack, or an edible portrait made of sugar and frosting.

Photos by Entertain!, Amanda May Photos, and Davello.

3. Tablescapes

Whether it's on welcome tables, gift tables, or dining tables, we love how our couples have found creative ways to showcase their pets through signage and decor. Printed cocktail napkins would pair well with your custom drinks, and you can't beat a hand-painted sign like Jessica and Patrick's.

And how cute are these place card holders from Amy and Tyler's wedding? They incorporated their lovable golden doodles into almost every aspect of their day, but we loved the way they greeted guests as they found their seats. You could also put an image of your pet on the place cards themselves or even on the dinner menu.

Photos by Walker Leigh, Amanda May Photos, and Julie Paisley

4. Inspired Signature Cocktails

All pets have unique personalities, so why not use them as the inspiration behind a custom wedding cocktail!

Do you have a sophisticated cat with a sassy side? Go with a martini with a twist.

Have a sweet and loyal lab who's starting to get some grays? Try a comforting old fashioned with honey.

Make sure you talk with your wedding planner or a skilled bartender—we're partial to the Nashville Event Bartending team—and you're sure to find the right combination of spirits and flavors. Don't forget to include an illustration of the cocktail's namesake on your bar signage!

Photos by  John Myers, Teale Photography, Wind & Gold Photography,  Amanda May Photos, and Frozen Exposure.

6. Pawsitively Radiant Lighting

Put your pet in your lighting design! Megan and Broughton included their pup in their custom gobo, and Kateylyn and Zach put the spotlight on their adorable cat. This way, even if your pet isn't physically in attendance, you'll feel like they're a part of the action.

Photos by Shots by Cheyenne and Details Nashville.

7. Apparel

Why not put your little angel right on your wedding day fit for everyone to see? Custom printed socks are always a great wedding day gift for a fun-loving groom. Our 2020 bride Megan scored big when she found a pair of Kate Spade dog sneakers that looked just like her little Boston Terrier, or you go with a custom-made accessory like this beaded purse if you've got more of a DIY aesthetic.

Photos by Frozen Exposure, Shots by Cheyenne and Rebecca Renee.

LET'S GET PERSONAL

OUR FAVORITE UNIQUE PERSONALIZED WEDDING TOUCHES

Couples are looking for more and more ways to incorporate their individual personalities into their weddings to have more than just a beautiful day with impeccable style. Say goodbye to tradition because your wedding can be timeless and unique.

Read on for some of our favorite ways to incorporate unique personalized touches into your big day.

1. Custom Illustrations

Whether it's a portrait of you and your spouse-to-be, your furry friends or even your venue, custom illustrations are a unique way to have a little fun and incorporate personal details into your wedding day.

Photos by Wind & Gold, Davello, John Myers Photography, and Details Nashville

2. Fun Fact Cocktail Napkins

Personalized cocktail napkins are a fun way to give your guests a glimpse into your union. Plus, you might be surprised what some guests don't know!

Photo by Courtney Davidson

3. Alternative Guest Book

A wedding guest book is a must-have, but the traditional book is often something that ends up collecting dust on a shelf. We’re loving when couples opt for something that speaks to who they are, like a pair of their favorite records, a globe, or a guitar. Bonus? These keepsakes can be prominently displayed in your home after your wedding and admired for years to come.

Photos by Details Nashville and Molly Peach

4. Name Lettering

Not only is a marquee sign a fun way to light up a space, they’re also a trendy way to showcase your newly-shared last name. Other ways we love incorporating a couple’s name into their big day include custom gobo lights shining on the floor or wall or customized wooden signs.

Photos by Wind & Gold and Details Nashville

5. Thoughtful Wedding Favors

Whether it’s a donation to your favorite charity in honor of your guests or a mixtape of your favorite songs, wedding favors are guaranteed to add a personal touch to your day.

Photo by BlueGenes Photography

6. Custom Style

Embroidered jackets, hand-written notes on the soles of your shoes, custom veils... you name it! Dress to impress on the big day by incorporating your unique personal style. While some traditions can be sentimental, don’t fall into the pressure of what you “should” wear. Express yourself!

Photos by Walker Leigh, John Myers Photography, and Rebecca Denton
