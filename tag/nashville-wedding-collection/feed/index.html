Ashley and Justin celebrated their marriage with a romantic blush wedding at The Bridge Building.

These two met at work and knew it was love. Because Ashley is hard to surprise, Justin proposed after she had just gone to the eye doctor and her eyes were still dilated. Since it was the only way he knew he could pull it off!

From the first time we spoke, they knew they wanted a beautifully elegant wedding. And loved the idea of ethereal draping and lots of fresh florals.

The Cumberland space was transformed into an undeniably romantic room, with ballet slipper pink and snow white florals by PS Designs covering almost every surface.

Since they wanted the space to feel as upscale as possible, they opted for a stunning floral ring suspended with blush draping above the altar. It later served as a beautiful focal point for the reception hanging over the head table.

It was an absolutely beautiful day and as a result, Ashley and Justin utilized the riverfront patio and its jaw-dropping backdrop for their picturesque first look.

After a heartfelt ceremony, guests enjoyed a sunny cocktail hour on the patio. Where they sipped margaritas, the couple's signature cocktail, and martinis from Infinity's specialty martini bar.

Light bites included tomato bisque and mini grilled cheese, candied bacon, and maple bacon chicken skewers. Because you can truly never have enough bacon!

Shortly after, guests were served a plated dinner. Entrees included bourbon-glazed salmon served with sautéed haricots vert and sweet potato praline casserole. As well as maple mustard-glazed chicken served with roasted brussels sprouts and caramelized onion and bacon whipped potatoes.

To wrap up, Ashley tossed her bouquet out on the riverfront. And boy was it a fun spot to tick off that wedding tradition!

We love that the photos captured not only the moment but the entire Nashville skyline as well. It's such a great spot for bride's to use!
