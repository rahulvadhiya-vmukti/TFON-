Today we're wishing Vaishali and Daniel a Happy Five Year Anniversary! Their vibrant Indian wedding at The Bell Tower was what sweet summer wedding dreams are made of.

Vaishali looked breathtaking in a tradition red bridal lehenga with gold accessories and ornate henna on her hands and feet. She later changed into an equally stunning black and gold sari.

For the ceremony, Vaishali and Daniel were joined by their parents under a mandap adorned with greenery and roses by Rosemary & Finch. The couple exchanged vibrant floral garlands, jaimalas or varmalas, symbolizing the welcoming of each other into their families.

After their traditional Indian wedding ceremony, it was time to celebrate! Guests enjoyed hors d'oeuvres and dinner from celebrity chef and Nashville resident Maneet Chauhan, known for her appearances on the Food Network. For dessert guests indulged in a decadent three-tiered cake and satisfied their sweet tooth with a candy bar.

As a nod to Daniel's profession as a dentist, their wedding favors were toothbrushes! Sitting not-so-subtly on the candy bar.

Congratulations to these two on five years! Keep scroll for some vibrant Indian wedding inspiration.
