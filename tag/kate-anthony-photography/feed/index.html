Lauren and Andrew were inspired by the clean architectural lines of The Bridge Building. With their use of a classic neutral color palette, their minimal modern wedding was the epitome of chic.

Taupe bridesmaids gowns paired with voluminous white bouquets complimented Lauren's sleek wedding gown, and dark, rich florals were placed atop elegant black linens bringing a sense of understated drama to the space.

Following a beautiful rooftop ceremony, guests headed down to the first floor for cocktail hour where they delighted in delicious appetizers including fried green tomatoes, mini chicken and waffles, and of course - shrimp and grits!

Following cocktail hour, guests were served a plated dinner of lean brisket with blackberry BBQ sauce, corn cakes, pear and apple slaw, and grilled balsamic vegetable skewers.

With full bellies and hearts, guests then headed upstairs to the rooftop for an assortment of donuts fromÂ Donut Den for dessert, and to dance the night away in celebration of Lauren and Andrew.
