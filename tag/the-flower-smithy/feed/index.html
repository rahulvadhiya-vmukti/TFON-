Kendra and Logan celebrated their love with a contemporary Christmas wedding ceremony and reception at The Bell Tower.

Cascading string lights and candlelit tables, filled the space with an unforgettably warm ambiance. While gray watercolor, silver accents, and arrangements of evergreen added a contemporary feel to this holiday wedding.

Following a sweet ceremony between the windows, guests headed to the patio to snack on fruit and cheese while the main space was flipped for the reception.

Before wedding party introductions, guests took their seats for dinner. Preset custom apple and cranberry salads were waiting at each place setting. Entrees included ginger soy salmon and herb encrusted chicken with grilled asparagus and parmesan risotto.

For their first dance, the blushing couple danced to Tom Odell's "Grow Old With Me." A song they felt was "so sweet and so perfect." We thought so too!

Later in the evening, guests enjoyed a night of fun and dancing hosted by Entertain! while they sipped on their preference of refreshments.

As a parting gift, Kendra and Logan gave their guests Christmas tree seedlings for them to take home and grow. How sweet!
