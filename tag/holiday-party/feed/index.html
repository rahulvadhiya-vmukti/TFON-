We helped Google throw an Edgar Allan Poe-inspired masquerade party at The Bridge Building with a spooky twist.

The event was held to bring everyone together in celebration of their milestones from the year and allow the team to have a great time! 

To start, we dressed up the main space with velvet linens, melted candles, and loads of greenery. In addition, the chandeliers were covered in webs and red LED uplighting by Nashville Lighting and Production filled the space, amplifying all the spooky vibes.

As guests arrived for cocktail hour, they were greeted with delectable hors d'oeuvres with a stationed dinner to follow. Dinner selections included a trio of salads, antipasto, porchetta, and fried ravioli. 

As guests munched and mingled, they were able to join in on a few casino games provided by FADDS Entertainment, as well as grab a signature cocktail called the Seven Sangria— which was so on theme, as Edgar Allan Poe included seven colored rooms into his short story "The Masque of The Red Death."
