LAUREN + WADE’S BOHEMIAN SUMMER WEDDING

A dreamy bohemian wedding with rustic touches at The Bell Tower.

Lauren and Wade's vision for their day mirrored who they are as a couple. Lauren dreamed of having a natural, bohemian summer wedding with rich florals and delicious food. And that is just what she got.

Coming from a big Greek family, the bride incorporated many fun Greek traditions into their wedding day from Greek desserts to line dancing. This unique touch added so much to their big day.

Following the ceremony, the Infinity team flipped the room for a buffet-style dinner. Each table was finished perfectly with beautiful burgundy napkins, lush greenery, and custom menus. At each place setting, guests received a small piece of Nashville flair, a Loveless Cafe guitar pick to take home.

For dessert, instead of your traditional stacked wedding cake, the couple opted for a variety of miniature pies and dessert bites from Sweet Creations. Lauren and Wade's guests loved it! Their four-legged fur babies were replicated to create the most adorable topper. It was a great touch for this dog loving couple.

After dinner, their family and friends enjoyed samplings of over 100 different whiskeys and bourbons in The Bell Tower's Tasting Room. Meanwhile others hit the dance floor for an epic dance party. Music City Rhythm Revue had the dance floor packed for the majority of the evening. It was a hit!
