A Baseball-Themed Bar Mitzvah

We had such a fun time celebrating Alex with this baseball themed bar mitzvah at OZ Arts Nashville. He had everything from grass table runners to inflatable baseball games to chain link fencing for décor. Even the dance floor was personalized to look like a baseball field, and Nashville Audio Visual created a marquee sign to read "Alex's Dugout."

Likewise, the menu was completely customized by the Infinity Hospitality culinary team to incorporate baseball snack essentials like Cracker Jacks and Coca-Cola.

Kids picked from a selection of mini hot dogs with a variety of toppings like mac n' cheese and caramel fried jalapeño cracker jacks, as well as mini pretzels with cheese and build-your-own nachos. Plus, there was an ice cream sundae bar for dessert.

While the kids snacked on mini bites, the adults enjoyed a plated dinner with many fun twists on traditional baseball game eats. The meal started with a salad of mixed greens topped with boiled peanuts and a Cracker Jack vinaigrette. For the main entree, guests chose from Coca-Cola brined pretzel-crusted chicken or cheddar encrusted filet with a whole grain mustard horseradish sauce. Some of the snack inspired sides included fire roasted creamed corn, popcorn risotto, and beer roasted brussels sprouts.

Overall, the entire event was a blast! Scroll for more details of the day.
