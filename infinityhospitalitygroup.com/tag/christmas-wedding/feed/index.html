Lovers of Christmas, Melissa and Sutton proudly embraced the holiday season and spirit for their winter wedding.

Their day began with a traditional ceremony at the Cathedral of the Incarnation followed by a fun-filled reception at The Bell Tower.

After the ceremony, guests were treated to a decadent cocktail hour including tomato bisque and miniature grilled cheeses, shrimp and grits bites, hot chicken sliders, candied bacon, and more.

The father of the bride kicked off the celebration with a speech and an amazing surprise a cappella performance group singing “I'll Be There.”

The luxurious feel of an elegant white wonderland filled the room with beautiful personal touches throughout. Custom ornaments were sent home as party favors, and the groom's favorite sport was showcased through a delicious groom's cake in the shape of a golfball.

The evening was complete with coffee, dessert, and dancing. When the celebration came to a close, Melissa and Sutton made their way off into the night with sweet sparkler sendoff.
