We helped Mary Keith and Austin kick off their wedding festivities with a sweet, blue summer rehearsal dinner at The Bell Tower.

The couple brought the summer sky inside The Bell Tower by pairing its classic and historic lines with a fun and inviting color palette of Carolina blue and white. A fresh, mini baguette was placed at each setting accompanied by whimsical hand calligraphed name cards by White Ink Calligraphy.

For dinner, guests were treated to chef-carved slow roasted prime rib with grilled squash, zucchini wedges, and rosemary roasted new potatoes. After dinner guests enjoyed cheesecake with fresh strawberries for dessert. And the party didn't stop there!

Mary Keith and Austin were joined by a hundred more of their guests as their after party began. Guests snacked on a late night bite of delicious beef sliders and danced the night away to celebrate this wonderful couple and their upcoming nuptials.
