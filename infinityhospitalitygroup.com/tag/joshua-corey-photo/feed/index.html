Amiyah and Kaleb had their floral-filled spring vow renewal ceremony and reception at The Bell Tower, complete with classic white decor and gorgeous greenery.

The couple met in 2018 through a friend's Instagram and Kaleb insisted on a date. Amiyah, being a true crime fanatic, had no interest in going on a date with some random guy. Kaleb, being the true gentleman he is, ended up taking out both Amiyah and her friend! He wined and dined them and even brought flowers. After the second date, just the two of them, they both knewâ€¦ and the rest is history.

On their wedding day, Amiyah wore a stunning three-piece dress set that included a stretch bodysuit and skirt slip. The fit-and-flare gown featured an illusion lace overdress with beaded embroidery and a deep V-neckline, complete with a fitted skirt with panels of chiffon and a chapel train! She held a gorgeous cascading bouquet of white roses, eucalyptus, and greenery that beautifully matched the rest of her day. Kaleb looked dapper in his military uniform, while his groomsmen wore white shirts with coordinating ties that matched the bridesmaids' light pink dresses.

For the ceremony, the main space of The Bell Tower was set with soft string lighting, white flowers, greenery, and white petals lining the aisle with classic dark wood chiavari chairs, plus a striking gold ceremony arch decorated with more white blooms between the grand windows.

The vow renewal was full of fun and sweet personal moments, like when Amiyah was walked down the aisle by her grandmother and the wedding party danced their way to cocktail hour!

After the couple said their "I dos" again in front of family and friends, the main space was seamlessly flipped for their beautiful reception. The stunning gold floral arch remained the focal point of the room as the head table was set in front of it. Tables were set with white linens and white floral centerpieces with greenery that popped against the minimalist but classic design. The head table was also decorated with floating candles and garlands of greenery down the center.
