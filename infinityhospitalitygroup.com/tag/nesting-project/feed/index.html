Tracey and Eddie said "I do" under an arch of fresh greenery at The Bell Tower. From ribbon wands to table linens, their southern wedding was navy and blush from head to toe. This is one of our favorite color palettes. The contrasting shades provide that perfect balance of masculine and feminine.

This couple really embraced the south, encouraging their guests to "take a taste of Tennessee." From southern bites of fried mac and cheese, hot chicken sliders, and candied bacon deviled eggs to GooGoo Clusters and MoonPies as parting gifts. This was a southern wedding at its finest.

Following the ceremony and cocktail hour, everyone was treated to a plate dinner. Guests had the option of house smoked brisket with grilled asparagus and cheddar and chive smashed potatoes or maple mustard glazed chicken with southern style green beans and rosemary roasted new potatoes.

See below for more details of their day and photos from the wonderful Details Nashville!
