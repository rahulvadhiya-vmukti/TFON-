Talor and AJ celebrated their marriage with a timeless wedding day at the historic Bell Tower filled with classic style, black decor, and white florals.

The two met when Talor took her little brother to baseball practice and AJ was the coach. They hit it off, and eventually AJ proposed in Talor’s backyard after a Titans game.

The couple started their wedding day with a ceremony at The Bell Tower with glamorous chandeliers, soft string lighting, dramatic black drapes, and white flower arrangements with greenery, creating a timeless and classic wedding ceremony design with moody vibes.

Talor stunned in a beautiful strapless column gown with a chapel train and large bow in the back. She wore her hair in a sleek low bun with pearl earrings and tied it all together with elegant fingerless gloves and a classic white hand-tied bouquet. Talor's bridesmaids wore stunning, one-shoulder black gowns and held matching bouquets. While the guys wore sharp black tuxedos from Jos A Banks.

After Talor and AJ said "I do," our main space was flipped for the reception. The room was set with a stage in the corner and a traditional king's table with space to dance in the middle, surrounded by round tables dressed in black linens with black chiavari chairs and chandeliers above. There was also a beautiful white neon sign customized with their newly-shared last name, which was the perfect way to highlight the chic couple.

Talor and AJ shared a sweet first dance to “Another” by Adam Doleac, and as the evening went on, Talor had a wardrobe change into an above-the-knee reception dress with white cowboy boots to ensure she could dance the night away! T-Rac, the Tennessee Titans mascot, even made a guest appearance and hit the dance floor with Talor and AJ’s loved ones! Such a fun nod to their engagement!

The newlyweds made their exit as loved ones cheered and waved neon streamers. Scroll on for more details of Talor and AJ’s timeless black wedding at The Bell Tower.
