Kaitlin and Shae envisioned a magical winter wonderland for their November wedding at The Bridge Building. With a crisp color palette of white and icy blue mixed with clear glass accents, they achieved just that.

As their romantic rooftop ceremony commenced, the sun began to set providing the perfect ambience. Kaitlin's brother led the ceremony and was ordained specifically to hold this honor on his sister's special day.

Following the ceremony, guests were invited downstairs to continue with a cocktail-style reception. They were treated to a variety of savory bites including brisket tacos, margherita flatbread, and southern chicken sliders.

For dessert, delectable miniature sweets were up for grabs and the groom's showstopper cake (a tiered stack of Krispy Kreme donuts) was a crowd favorite.

Guests had a blast dancing the night away, and were even treated to an impromptu karaoke performance by the happy couple. It was truly a magical celebration, with love and donuts for all.
