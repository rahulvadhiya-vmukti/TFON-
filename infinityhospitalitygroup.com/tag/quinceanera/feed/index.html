Well, one thing is for certain, and that is this birthday girl's favorite color is pink!

We were so excited to help Andrea throw her perfectly pink quinceañera at The Bridge Building.

White linens with candy pink napkins from Liberty Party Rental kept the space pretty and bright. Lush pink and white orchids from Branching Out floated in clear glass votives with pink rose petals strewn across the tables. Elegant gold accents added extra flair.

For dinner, guests sat down to pre-set Southwest wedge salads. They were then served chicken cordon bleu with grilled asparagus and herbed basmati rice.

But above all, the show-stopper of the party was the jaw-dropping dessert table by Yesica Legorreta. It was truly opulent and sure to satisfy any sweet tooth.

This was the first quinceañera I'd ever been to, so watching Andrea perform all of the traditional dances with her family was such a fun treat! She even choreographed the surprise dance with her quinceañera court herself. It was nice to learn about another culture's traditions.

Happy 15th birthday, Andrea!
