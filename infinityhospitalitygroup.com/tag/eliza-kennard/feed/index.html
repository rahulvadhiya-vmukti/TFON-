Robin and Will had an elegant holiday wedding ceremony and reception at The Bridge Building.

After ten years as friends and three years as a couple, Will finally got down on one knee at Cheekwood. Exactly one year after Robin said, "yes," the happy couple said, "I do!"

It was important to Robin and Will that their guests had an enjoyable time during their wedding day.

Cocktail hour started pre-ceremony which led to Robin and Will's most memorable moment; "turning around at the ceremony and seeing everyone with a drink in hand, toasting to the new bride and groom!"

Robin and Will kicked off their reception with a first dance to "Signed, Sealed, Delivered," by Stevie Wonder. The room was adorned with soft neutrals and simple navy touches. While candlelit centerpieces with arrangements of greenery gave the space the winter vibe Robin and Will were counting on.

When the night was over, the couple opted out of the traditional send-off. Instead, they had all of their guests join them in one last dance to, "This Will Be," by Natalie Cole. How fun!
