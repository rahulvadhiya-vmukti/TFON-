Did you hear the news? This Beyonce-loving bride and her new hubby went viral! Allie knew she found the one when Tyler passed The Official Allie Davis Relationship Test. The test featured a series of questions about the one and only Bey and some of her other favorite pop artists.

Shortly after she posted the results to her Twitter page, it was an absolute hit, and people start following along with their relationship.When Tyler finally decided to "put a ring on it," he proposed under the traditional arches on their college campus back in Oxford, Ohio.

Allie and Tyler tied the knot with a romantic wedding at The Bell Tower filled with personality, and of course, plenty of Bey. These two wanted a classic, romantic feel with colors of black, white, and gold incorporated into their day. But most importantly, they wanted to include their fun personalities.

"We did little video interludes to give background on our relationship and give guests an idea of what was coming next," Allie told us. The videos were all inspired by Beyonce concerts.

But that wasn't the only thing Beyonce-inspired. Allie's mom made personalized candles for their guests with a beehive on it. "It was a way of inducting our guests into the Beyhive," Allie told us.

As for the music, it was basically Bey only. During the ceremony, Tyler walked down the aisle to “Halo,” followed by Allie's staircase entrance to “Ave Maria." The wedding party was then introduced into the reception to “Crazy In Love.”

To end a memorable day of laughter and dancing, Allie and Tyler sang "Piano Man," with their college friends before running through a sparkler lit tunnel.

For images of their Beyonce-inspired wedding, keep scrolling! To read more on what made them go viral, check it all out on Buzzfeed and The Huff Post.
