Elissa and Karlo’s said “I do” with the dreamiest rustic wedding at The Bridge Building, designed with romantic details and modern style.

For the ceremony, these two chose the tented rooftop patio that overlooks downtown Nashville. The aisle was lined with ivory rose petals that led to a custom wood altar adorned with colorful florals. Rustic lighting complemented the whole aesthetic.

Karlo saw Elissa for the first time as she walked down the aisle in her lace Rita Vinieris gown. Opting out of a first look made the ceremony their most memorable moment of the day. And in addition to exchanging vows, they decided to braid a rope to signify their forever bone as they began this new chapter of life together!

Once the ceremony was over, guests headed down to the riverfront to enjoy a few savory bites while taking in a dreamy sunset. Once guests went back inside for the reception, they found their seats and a plated dinner service began. The main course was a choice between bourbon-glazed salmon, prime rib, or a maple pork chop, and sides included grilled asparagus, buttermilk mashed potatoes, roasted acorn squash, and vegetable hash.

Later in the evening, Elissa and Karlo shared a first dance to "Change Your Name" by Brett Young before cutting into their dreamy wedding cake, which was at the center of table filled with donuts! 

After a night of celebration, an unforgettable sparkler exit, and lots of love, Elissa and Karlo's wedding day was complete!
