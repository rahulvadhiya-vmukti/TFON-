Aside from some of the more practical reasons to have a brunch wedding, like a morning venue rental is more budget-friendly, what's more fun than celebrating your nuptials over the best meal of the week? Brunch!

From interactive DIY stations to coffee carts and donut cakes, a brunch wedding is a more casual but festive way to commemorate your "I dos."

1. Donuts

Pull up your Pinterest boards! What better way to incorporate that donut wall you've been eyeing than at a brunch wedding? Our go-to at Infinity is the one and only Fox's Donut Den! Did you know that was our owner's very first job? Whether you're a Nashville native or a recent transplant, Donut Den is a crowd favorite.

2. Mimosas

Take it to the next level with Nashville Event Bartending's make-your-own mimosa bar. With sparkling white wine and a variety of juices like OJ, cranberry juice and grapefruit juice, this is such fun way to impress your guests.

3. Biscuits

Welcome to the South! What's better than a southern biscuit bar? With sausage patties, bacon, country ham, pulled pork bbq and fried chicken bites, there's a biscuit for everybody.

4. Cereal

Sometimes you just want a bowl of Lucky Charms, am I right? A fun DIY cereal bar is another great way to get your guests moving and mingling.
