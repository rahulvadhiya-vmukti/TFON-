It was a gorgeous spring day for a gorgeous couple. An southern vintage wedding where no detail was left to chance.

The Bridge Building was filled with vintage furniture from Southern Events to encapsulate their elegant theme. Hints of vintage lace and accents of gray, pink, and navy surrounded the reception and ceremony.

As guests entered, they were offered Erin & Seth's signature cocktail, the Blackberry Sparkler. It was a refreshing mix of blackberry liqueur and sparkling wine topped with a fresh blackberry. As a guest book, the couple used their favorite records. Guests wrote their sweet notes and congratulations in silver and gold on the record sleeves.

The couple's ceremony on the riverfront overlooking the gorgeous skyline of Nashville was an absolute stunner.

Following the ceremony, guests enjoyed cocktail hour and delighted in small bites including bacon wrapped scallops, maple bacon chicken skewers, and mini pulled pork biscuit sliders.

After mingling, family and friends sat down for a plated dinner. The meal started with a balsamic grape and apple salad. Guests indulged in peppercorn filet and prawn with grilled asparagus and garlic parmesan mashed potatoes as the main course.
