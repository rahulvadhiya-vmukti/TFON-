The Bell Tower provided the perfect setting for Kristen and Guido’s romantic, flower-filled wedding reception. Decorated with delicate gold details, lush floral arrangements in hues of white and pink with verdant greenery, the soft glow of candles, and white draping from the beams, their celebration exuded elegance.

But before we dive into the splendor of their reception, let’s rewind to where it all began – the love story of Kristen and Guido. Childhood sweethearts, their bond stood the test of time and distance, only to be rekindled later in life.

Guido whisked Kristen away to his native country, Italy, where amid the breathtaking beauty of Lake Como, he asked her to be his forever with the help of his parents.

Fast forward to their wedding day, where the Bell Tower served as a symbol of their enduring love and the beginning of their new chapter together.

As guests entered the main space, they were greeted by cascading white drapery with a grand chandelier at the center, and rose-filled floral arrangements with the delicate scent of pink and white blooms mingling with the warm glow of candles.

During cocktail hour, guests sipped on signature cocktails— an Oaxaca Old Fashioned and Raspberry Moscow Mules —while eating an array of hors d’oeuvres made by the in-house culinary team. Selections included candied bacon, Key West crab cakes, and classic tomato bruschetta.

They then found their seats at tables set with elegant gold accents and a mix of smaller floral arrangements in bud vases, with textured pink napkins and custom menu cards at each place setting. They dined on a balsamic caprese salad and their choice of filet mignon, rigatoni primavera, or pan seared dayboat fish.

Amid laughter and heartfelt toasts, Kristen and Guido danced the night away, their love shining brighter than ever. Surrounded by family and friends, they celebrated not only their union but also the journey that brought them to this moment of joy and happiness.

As the evening drew to a close, the newlyweds left the Bell Tower hand in hand, ready to embark on the adventure of marriage together.

May Kristen and Guido’s love continue to bloom and flourish and their journey together be filled with countless moments of joy, laughter, and unwavering love!

Deysi, originally from Brazil, and Michael, from New Jersey, first met at work when Deysi moved to New Jersey. Starting off as just friends, Michael showed Deysi the ropes when she made the big move. Their love grew slowly but surely, and after another move to Nashville, these two were ready to tie the knot.

With most of their guests coming from out of town, and many from Brazil, they wanted to make sure it was a smooth and enjoyable experience for all. It was important that the food, drinks, and atmosphere were top tier— and with us, that’s a promise! The Estate was the perfect backdrop for their guests to celebrate with its breathtaking views of Old Hickory Lake and sprawling lawns. A truly memorable and magical experience.

Deysi and Michael got ready with their own wedding parties in the mansion’s luxury bridal suite and nearby Stable Loft  before walking the aisle to say “I do” on the Lakeside Lawn. The lakeside ceremony was set with arrangements of vibrant blooms and lush greenery at the altar with a flower petal aisle.

Afterwards, guests sipped on glasses of frozé with a selection of passed apps during the outdoor cocktail hour in the Serenity Gardens, including Bloody Mary oysters, hot chicken biscuits, ahi tuna in a crispy wonton, arancini, polenta cakes with goat cheese, and Key West crab cakes. Lounge areas were also set to create a comfortable environment for guests to mingle with string lights for a fairytale ambiance.

The room was draped in forest green velvet with strings of fairy lights across the whole ceiling, while tables were set with soft white linens and acrylic ghost chairs. Centerpieces included antique candelabras wrapped in greenery and full-size faux trees with floating glass bulbs, individually hung white flowers, tea lights, and touches of moss. It was like a fairy tale brought to life!

For dinner, we helped Deysi and Michael curate a culinary experience that embraced Nashville’s renowned food scene. The menu featured a fusion of Southern flavors and personal favorites with a selection of four entrees—lemon almondine trout, grilled lamb chop with a tomato demi-glace, chicken cordon bleu, and rigatoni primavera.

During the reception, live musicians played celebratory tunes to kick off the dance floor  and encourage loved ones to join the couple in the fun. And after a late night dessert display of French toast bread pudding, mini cheesecake bites, cake cutting, and more, Deysi and Michael ended their night with a jump in the pool!
