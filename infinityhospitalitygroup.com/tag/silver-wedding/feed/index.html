Alina, a Radio City Christmas Spectacular Rockette, and her husband to be, Geoff Silver, set out to have a silver destination wedding in Nashville. And that they did.

Their wedding weekend began with a rehearsal dinner at The Bell Tower, where Alina surprised her groom with a tap dance routine. What else would you expect from a rockette?

For the ceremony and reception, The Bridge Building Event Spaces was decked out in silver head-to-toe. From silver sequined linens to crystal curtains to the "glitter girls" who walked down the aisle with a sparkling banner, it was all in the details.

The newlyweds were even sent off with biodegradable, silver ecofetti.

As seen on Modern Jewish Wedding, Bitchless Bride and The Big Fat Jewish Wedding, this silver destination wedding was a blast! And the bride's silver, beaded gown was to die for.
