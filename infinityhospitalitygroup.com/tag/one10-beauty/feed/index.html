Hannah and Justin said their “I dos” with a stunning ceremony between the grand arched windows at The Bell Tower and a reception decorated with romantic, rose-filled arrangements and cascading string lights. 

This couple met in 2017 through mutual friends in Music City. When Justin worked up the courage to ask Hannah out (two years later), they enjoyed a nice dinner and were soon inseparable.  

Justin popped the question while vacationing with Hannah’s family in Saint Simons. With the help of their new puppy, Kunu, Justin placed a new bandana on him saying, “Will you marry my dad?” 

On their wedding day, Hannah and Justin shared a sweet first look outside of the Bell Tower. Hannah had a slick-back bun with pearl studded earrings and a classic ball gown for her romantic winter bridal style. Her bridesmaids were in matching black silk dresses, and as for Justin, he went with an all black tux and burgundy boutonniere. 

For the ceremony, the main space of The Bell Tower was decorated with soft cascading string lights, a glamorous chandelier hanging from above, bright red rose aisle markers, and a rose-filled arrangement covering the ceremony arch, creating the most romantic ambiance.  The grand staircase was adorned with an abundance of greenery that ended with glowing pillar candles at the bottom and a blooming rose arrangement that matched Hannah’s bouquet.

After the ceremony, guests took to the patio for a cocktail hour of wine and candied bacon while Infinity staff flipped the main space for the reception. 

The main space was transformed with classic round tables and separate seating for the wedding party. The romantic color palette was incorporated into every aspect of their setup with beautiful,elevated floral arrangements, full of more blooming roses, set on white linens with black napkins and pillar candles to create a warm glow. 

After Hannah and Justin’s first dance to  “To Make You Feel My Love” by Garth Brooks, guests enjoyed a plated dinner service of honey balsamic chicken with roasted brussel sprouts and goat cheese polenta, bourbon glazed salmon with grilled asparagus and parmesan risotto, and smoked brisket with garlic parmesan mashed potatoes and grilled corn. 

During the reception, Hannah and Justin opted to include a whiskey tasting in The Bell Tower’s Tasting Room so guests could sip on fine spirits while celebrating the newlyweds.

After Hannah changed into a shorter white dress for the reception and danced the night away with Justin, the happy couple was sent on their newly married way!

Talor and AJ celebrated their marriage with a timeless wedding day at the historic Bell Tower filled with classic style, black decor, and white florals.

The two met when Talor took her little brother to baseball practice and AJ was the coach. They hit it off, and eventually AJ proposed in Talor’s backyard after a Titans game.

The couple started their wedding day with a ceremony at The Bell Tower with glamorous chandeliers, soft string lighting, dramatic black drapes, and white flower arrangements with greenery, creating a timeless and classic wedding ceremony design with moody vibes.

Talor stunned in a beautiful strapless column gown with a chapel train and large bow in the back. She wore her hair in a sleek low bun with pearl earrings and tied it all together with elegant fingerless gloves and a classic white hand-tied bouquet. Talor's bridesmaids wore stunning, one-shoulder black gowns and held matching bouquets. While the guys wore sharp black tuxedos from Jos A Banks.

After Talor and AJ said "I do," our main space was flipped for the reception. The room was set with a stage in the corner and a traditional king's table with space to dance in the middle, surrounded by round tables dressed in black linens with black chiavari chairs and chandeliers above. There was also a beautiful white neon sign customized with their newly-shared last name, which was the perfect way to highlight the chic couple.

Talor and AJ shared a sweet first dance to “Another” by Adam Doleac, and as the evening went on, Talor had a wardrobe change into an above-the-knee reception dress with white cowboy boots to ensure she could dance the night away! T-Rac, the Tennessee Titans mascot, even made a guest appearance and hit the dance floor with Talor and AJ’s loved ones! Such a fun nod to their engagement!

The newlyweds made their exit as loved ones cheered and waved neon streamers. Scroll on for more details of Talor and AJ’s timeless black wedding at The Bell Tower.
