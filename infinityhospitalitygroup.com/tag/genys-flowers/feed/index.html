Lauren and Oscar's stylish spring wedding at The Bridge Building was light, bright and unapologetically dreamy.

The couple chose an elegant, neutral color palette of white and gold, with a hint of greenery here and there. Delicate white florals by Geny's Flowers were perfectly whimsical and added plenty of romance wherever they were placed.

The day began with an intimate rooftop ceremony. Two tall floral arrangements filled with ivory and blush roses framed the altar. While sweet bundles of natural greenery were tied to ceremony seating with silky white ribbon.

As guests ventured downstairs for dinner, a number of food stations awaited hungry tummies. Guests indulged in Infinity's southern comforts station, which includes roasted sweet corn hush puppies, fried green tomatoes and of course, southern fried chicken sliders.

Guests sipped on classic margaritas by Nashville Event Bartending to stay cool and played a few games of corn hole down by the riverfront.

Following dinner, a dance party lit up the room. Lauren and Oscar's first dance was beautifully backlit by the Pedestrian Bridge, causing more than a few not-so-dry eyes!

For dessert, guests had a blast creating their own ice cream sundaes, courtesy of Infinity's super-fun DIY ice cream sundae bar!

Additionally, a stunning intricately detailed four-tiered white cake by Nashville Sweets made jaws drop and mouths water.

As the evening came to a close, Lauren and Oscar's nearest and dearest sent them off through a tunnel of sparklers. They shared one last kiss and hopped into a waiting classic black and silver Bentley, provided by Matchless Transportation, ending the evening in undeniable style.
