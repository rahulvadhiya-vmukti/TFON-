Jacqui and Caleb's classic wine red wedding at The Bell Tower was full of thoughtful details and so much love.

Their story began after meeting at a bar in Franklin and continued with a pedestrian bridge proposal right in Downtown Nashville.

For the big day, Jacqui was dressed in a classic white gown with an open back and an all-white rose bouquet by Creations by Debbie. Her bridesmaids wore long wine red dresses with a refined up-do.

Jacqui and Caleb began their day with a sweet first look outside of The Bell Tower before saying their vows between the tall arched windows. At the altar, sat a wood arch adorned with roses and sprigs of greenery.

After the ceremony, guests sipped on cocktails on the patio while we transformed the main space into their perfect fall reception. The tables were set with white and wine red linens, gold lanterns, and a balsamic grape and apple salad at each place setting.

Once guests found their seats, a delectable plated dinner service began. Dinner included bourbon-glazed salmon with grilled asparagus and parmesan risotto, as well as roasted pork loin with steamed broccoli and cheddar cheese mashed potatoes, and chicken piccata with grilled vegetables and rosemary roasted potatoes.

Jacqui and Caleb completed their day with a traditional bouquet and garter toss from the mezzanine before busting a move on the dance floor. For an extra thoughtful touch and to keep the kiddos busy, there were clipboards for coloring, too.
